How i Work ?

1. Design & UX in Figma +
I start by understanding the problem and translating ideas into structured wireframes and clean UI designs. Figma helps me explore layouts, user flows, and design systems efficiently while keeping everything collaborative and scalable.
2. Visual Enhancement with Photoshop +
When a project needs strong visuals, I use Photoshop to refine images, create mockups, enhance graphics, and maintain visual consistency across the design.
3. Build & Launch with Wix +
Once the design is finalized, I bring it to life using Wix. I carefully recreate the Figma design, apply interactions, optimize responsiveness, and ensure the website is ready for real users.
